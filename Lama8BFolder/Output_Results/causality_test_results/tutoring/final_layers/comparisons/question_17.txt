2.  Authentication: HTTPS verifies the identity of the website, ensuring that users are communicating with the intended website and not a fake or malicious one.
3.  Integrity: HTTPS ensures that data is not tampered with during transmission, preventing hackers from altering or manipulating sensitive information.
