1.  **Cool the burn**: Run cool tap water over the burn for 10-15 minutes or apply a cool, wet compress.
2.  **Remove clothing and jewelry**: Gently remove any clothing or jewelry near the burned area, as these can stick to the burn and cause further damage.
3.  **Apply a topical ointment**: Apply a topical antibiotic ointment to the burned area to help prevent infection.
4.  **Cover the burn**: Cover the burned area with a non-stick, sterile dressing or bandage to protect it from further irritation and infection.
