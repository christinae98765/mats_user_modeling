1. **Older adults:** As we age, our skin becomes less efficient at producing vitamin D from sunlight.

3. **Individuals with certain medical conditions:** Certain conditions, such as kidney or liver disease, may require vitamin D supplements.
4. **Pregnant or breastfeeding women:** Vitamin D is essential for fetal development and infant health.

**In conclusion:**
