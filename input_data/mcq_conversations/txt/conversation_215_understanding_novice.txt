Consider this recursive function:

def factorial(n):
    if n == 0:
        return 1
    return n * factorial(n - 1)

What is factorial(4)?

A) 10
B) 24
C) 16
D) 4

### Human:
C

### Assistant:
Not quite. factorial(4) = 4 * 3 * 2 * 1 = 24. Let's move on.
