C) Rex
D) Error

Consider this recursive function:
```python
def factorial(n):
    if n <= 1:
        return 1
    return n * factorial(n - 1)

print(factorial(4))
```
What is the output?
A) 24
B) 10
C) 4
D) 5

### Human:
A

### Assistant:
Correct. 4 * 3 * 2 * 1 equals 24.
